# DZip Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

## Introduction

DZip respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected and used when you use the DZip mobile game.

## Information We Collect

DZip may collect limited information including:

* Nicknames or player names
* Gameplay statistics and scores
* Leaderboard records
* Anonymous analytics and crash reports

DZip does not collect sensitive personal information such as real names, addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.

## How We Use Information

Collected information may be used to:

* Provide leaderboard functionality
* Save gameplay progress and streaks
* Improve gameplay experience
* Monitor app stability and fix bugs

## Data Sharing

DZip does not sell user data.

User data is not shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

## Data Security

All transmitted data is encrypted in transit using secure industry-standard protocols.

## Children's Privacy

DZip is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

## Data Retention

Some gameplay-related data may be stored temporarily for leaderboard and gameplay functionality. Old or inactive data may be deleted automatically over time.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
