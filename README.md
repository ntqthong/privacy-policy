# Privacy Policies for Mobile Apps and Games

This repository contains the privacy policy that applies to my mobile apps and games.

**Effective date:** 2026-05-27

## Privacy Policy

I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal data.

### 1. Information I Collect
Depending on the app or game, I may collect:
- **Device and usage data** (device model, OS version, app version, language, crash logs)
- **Identifiers** (advertising ID or app instance ID)
- **Gameplay and progress data** (scores, settings, in-game progress)
- **Purchase data** (purchase status or receipt validation data, but not your full payment details)

### 2. How Information Is Used
Collected information may be used to:
- Provide and maintain app/game features
- Save progress and improve gameplay experience
- Fix bugs, monitor performance, and prevent abuse
- Show ads (if applicable)
- Process and restore in-app purchases (if applicable)

### 3. Third-Party Services
Some apps/games may use trusted third-party services, such as:
- Analytics providers
- Crash reporting services
- Advertising networks
- App store billing and distribution platforms

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

### 4. Data Sharing
I do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared only:
- With service providers needed to operate the apps/games
- If required by law or legal process
- To protect rights, safety, and prevent fraud

### 5. Data Retention
I retain data only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

### 6. Children's Privacy
My apps and games are not intended to collect personal data from children knowingly. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact me so I can delete it.

### 7. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your data. You can request this by contacting me.

### 8. Security
I use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

### 9. Changes to This Policy
I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in this repository with a new effective date.

### 10. Contact
For privacy-related questions or requests, contact:
- **GitHub Issues:** https://github.com/ntqthong/privacy-policy/issues

---

If a specific app or game has additional privacy terms, those terms should be added as app-specific sections or files in this repository.
